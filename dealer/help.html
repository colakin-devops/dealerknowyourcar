Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new vehicle?

expand_more

To add a new vehicle, navigate to your Dashboard and click the 'Add Vehicle' button. You'll be prompted to enter the vehicle's details such as make, model, year, and VIN. Once you've filled out the required information, click 'Save'.

How do I manage my Car?

expand_more

Your car can be managed from the 'Dashboard'. Here you can view all vehicles, edit their details, or remove them. Use filters and search to find vehicles quickly.

How do I search for a vehicle?

expand_more

Go to the 'Search' page. You can search by VIN, make, model, or year. Advanced filters let you refine results for faster lookup.

How do I update my profile?

expand_more

Click your profile picture in the top-right corner and select 'Profile Settings'. You can update your details, password, and contact info there.

How do I contact support?

expand_more

You can reach our team via the 'Support' link in the sidebar, or email support@knowmytow.com. We aim to reply within 24 hours.
